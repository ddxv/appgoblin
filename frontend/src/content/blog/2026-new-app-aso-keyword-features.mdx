## AppGoblin now supports granular keyword tracking

The new ASO keywords dash on each app gives mobile app and game developers access to completely free tools to track their app's progress and growth on the app stores for specific keywords.

### What is new in the `/keywords` endpoint for each app

* You can now clearly see where each keyword came from:
	* Keywords your app is currently ranking for
	* Keywords found in your app metadata and description
	* Keywords you personally added to your tracked list
* Logged-in users can load the dashboard using only their own tracked keywords.
* Every keyword includes practical ASO signals to help you decide what to work on next:
	* Current rank and best recent rank
	* Opportunity and difficulty scores
	* Competitive context like how crowded the keyword is

### Why this matters for ASO teams

* Developers can separate app ranking keywords from discovery suggestions instantly.
* Marketers can keep a clean tracked list and compare movement over time.
* Teams can move from "what is ranking" to "what to optimize next" in one workflow.
